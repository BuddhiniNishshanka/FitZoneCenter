Breakfast

Scrambled Eggs with Spinach & Whole Wheat Toast


Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup fresh spinach (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 slice whole wheat toast

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add spinach and sauté for 1-2 minutes until wilted.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Pour eggs into the pan and stir gently until cooked.
  5. Serve with whole wheat toast.

Omelet with Mushrooms, Feta Cheese & Whole Wheat Toast


Ingredients

  • 2 whole eggs + 2 egg whites
  • ½ cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 30g feta cheese (crumbled)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 slice whole wheat bread
  • Salt & black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté mushrooms for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Whisk eggs and pour over the mushrooms.
  3. Cook for 2-3 minutes, then sprinkle feta cheese on top.
  4. Fold the omelet and cook for another minute.
  5. Serve with whole wheat toast.

Avocado Toast with Poach Eggs


Ingredients

  • 1 slice whole wheat bread
  • ½ avocado (mashed)
  • 1 egg (poached)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast the whole wheat bread.
  2. Spread mashed avocado on top, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Poach an egg (boil water, swirl, drop the egg in, cook for 3-4 mins).
  4. Place the poached egg on the avocado toast and sprinkel red pepper flakes.

Oatmeal with Chia Seeds, Banana & Protein Pwoder


Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup milk or water
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • ½ banana (sliced)
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook oats with milk/water over medium heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Stir in chia seeds and protein powder, then remove from heat.
  3. Top with banana slices and honey.

Protein Pancakes with honey


Ingredients

  • ½ cup oats (blended into flour)
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup milk or almond milk
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp honey for topping
  • Berries (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients into a smooth batter. (except honey)
  2. Heat a pan and cook small pancakes for 2 minutes per side.
  3. Top with fresh berries
  4. Drizzle with honey and serve.

Chia Pudding with Almond Milk and Berries


Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • ½ cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

Instructions

  1. Mix chia seeds, almond milk and honey in a jar.
  2. Let it sit for at least 4 hours or overnight in the fridge.
  3. Top with fresh berries before serving.

Grilled Chicken, Brown Rice and Broccoli


Ingredients

  • 150g chicken breast
  • ½ cup brown rice
  • 1 cup broccoli (steamed)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder and paprika

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  2. Grill on medium heat for 6-7 minutes per side.
  3. Cook brown rice as per package instructions.
  4. Steam broccoli for 5 minutes and drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Serve together.

Lentil Soup with Whole-Grain Bread


Ingredients

  • ½ cup lentils
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • ½ onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (diced)
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Whole-grain bread

Instructions

  1. Saute onion and garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add lentils, tomato, and vegetable broth.
  3. Simmer fot 20-25 minutes until lentils are soft.
  4. Serve with whole-grain bread.

Grilled Chicken with Roasted Sweet Potatoes


Ingredients

  • 150g chicken breast
  • 1 medium sweet potato (cubed)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper and paprika

Instructions

  1. Season chicken and grill for 6-7 minutes per side.
  2. Toss sweet potato cubes with olive oil, salt and paprika.
  3. Roast at 200°C (400°F) for 20-25 minutes.
  4. Serve together.

Baked Fish with Mashed Sweet Potatoes


Ingredients

  • 150g fish fillet
  • 1 medium sweet potato
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper and garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Season fish with salt, pepper and garlic powder.
  2. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Boil and mash sweet potato with a little live oil.
  4. Serve together.

Chicken Soup with Whole Wheat Bread


Ingredients

  • 150g chicken breast (shredded)
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • ½ cup carrots (diced)
  • ½ cup celery (diced)
  • ½ onion (chopped)
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 slice whole wheat bread

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot, sauté onion, garlic, carrots, and celery.
  2. Add chicken broth and shredded chicken.
  3. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve with whole wheat bread.

Baked Salmon with Quinoa and Asparagus


Ingredients

  • 150g salmon fillet
  • ½ cup quinoa
  • 1 cup asparagus
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder and lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Season salmon with salt, pepper, garlic powder and lemon juice.
  3. Bake for 15-20 mins.
  4. Cook quinoa.
  5. Drizzle asparagus with olive oil, season with salt and bake for 10 mins.
  6. Serve together.

Baked Cod with Garlic Butter and Roasted Cauliflower


Ingredients

  • 150g cod fillet
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 tsp butter (melted)
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • Salt, black pepper, and lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix butter, garlic, and lemon juice, and brush onto cod fillet.
  3. Bake for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Toss cauliflower with olive oil and roast for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve together.

Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry


Ingredients

  • 150g chicken breast
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, snow peas)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • ½ tsp ginger

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan add garlic and ginger.
  2. Add chicken and stir-fry for 5-6 mins until cooked.
  3. Add vegetables and soya sauce, stir-fry for another 3-4 mins.
  4. Serve hot.

Grilled Shimp with Roasted Zucchini


Ingredients

  • 150g shrimp (peeled)
  • 1 medium zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill or pan.
  2. Season shrimp with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  3. Grill shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Toss zucchini with olive oil, roast in the oven at 200°C (400°F) for 15 minutes.
  5. Serve together.
